Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

TL;DR: We don't collect, store, or track any personal data. Messages are encrypted end-to-end and automatically deleted after 24 hours. We can't read your messages even if we wanted to.

1. Data We DON'T Collect

  • ❌ No email addresses or phone numbers
  • ❌ No user accounts or profiles
  • ❌ No message content (encrypted end-to-end)
  • ❌ No IP address logging
  • ❌ No cookies or tracking pixels
  • ❌ No analytics or user behavior tracking

2. Data We Temporarily Store

  • ✅ Encrypted message ciphertext (deleted after 24 hours)
  • ✅ Public encryption keys (deleted when you leave)
  • ✅ Anonymous usernames (deleted when you leave)
  • ✅ Connection metadata for rate limiting (deleted after 1 hour)

3. How Encryption Works

All messages are encrypted on your device before being sent to our servers. We use:

  • ECDH P-256 for key exchange
  • AES-GCM-256 for message encryption
  • PBKDF2 with 600,000 iterations for key derivation

The server only sees encrypted data and cannot decrypt your messages.

4. Data Retention

  • Messages: Automatically deleted after 24 hours
  • User sessions: Deleted when you close the browser
  • Rate limiting data: Deleted after 1 hour
  • Server logs: Deleted after 7 days

5. Third-Party Services

We may use:

  • Cloudflare: For DDoS protection (they may see IP addresses)
  • Hosting provider: For server infrastructure

We don't use Google Analytics, Facebook Pixel, or any tracking services.

6. Your Rights

Since we don't store personal data, there's nothing to delete or export. You can:

  • Leave any room at any time
  • Clear your browser data to remove local encryption keys
  • Contact us if you have concerns

7. Security

We implement multiple security measures:

  • Rate limiting to prevent spam
  • IP-based connection limits
  • Automatic banning of abusive IPs
  • Regular security updates

8. Changes to This Policy

We'll update this policy if our practices change. Check this page periodically for updates.
